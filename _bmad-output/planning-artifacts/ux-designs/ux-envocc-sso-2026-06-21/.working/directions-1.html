Classic Civic. Deep maroon on cream parchment with a double-rule certificate frame and a star seal evokes a stately, long-standing public institution — the feel of an official document or ministry crest. Traditional serif wordmark and warm tones convey heritage and continuity, reassuring older or non-technical staff that this is the established, authentic office. The ceremonial framing is costly to imitate, raising the bar for phishers.
