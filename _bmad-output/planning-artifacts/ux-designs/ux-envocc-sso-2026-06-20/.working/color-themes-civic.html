Civic Register — Four Refined Palettes

A locked institutional direction for the Department of Environmental & Occupational Health single sign-on: deep navy primary, warm gold accent, white ground, serif wordmark. Four variants below stay inside the family but differ in navy temperature/depth, accent warmth, neutral undertone, and contrast intensity. Light mode only. Every text/background pairing meets WCAG AA.
